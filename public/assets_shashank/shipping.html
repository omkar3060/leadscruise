Digital Services Policy

Since LeadsCruise delivers digital AI services, physical shipping is not applicable. This policy explains how service access and digital delivery works for our AI-powered lead generation platform.

1. Service Delivery

  • Service is activated immediately upon successful payment verification.
  • Access credentials are sent to your registered email address within minutes.
  • All services are delivered digitally through our secure cloud platform.
  • No physical goods or materials are shipped as part of our service offering.

2. GST Invoice Delivery

  • GST invoices are automatically generated and emailed within 4â€“7 business days of payment.
  • Users can download invoices anytime via "My Billing History" in their dashboard.
  • Invoice delivery is handled electronically to reduce processing time.
  • Physical invoice copies are not mailed unless specifically requested.

3. Demo Access

  • Demo accounts are activated instantly using provided coupon codes.
  • Demo usage is limited to one day per user account.
  • Demo access provides full feature preview with limited data processing capacity.
  • No billing information is required for demo account activation.

4. Non-Delivery Issues

  • If access is not granted after payment, users should contact support immediately.
  • Payment verification issues are resolved within 2-4 hours during business hours.
  • Technical access problems are prioritized and resolved within 1 hour.
  • Backup access methods are provided for critical delivery failures.

5. No Physical Shipping

  • All services are digital; no physical goods are shipped or delivered.
  • Service access is provided through secure cloud-based platforms only.
  • No courier or postal services are involved in service delivery.
  • Users access all features through web-based interfaces and mobile applications.

6. Exchange Policy

  • Since services are digital, traditional product exchanges are not applicable.
  • Service modifications or feature adjustments can be requested through support.
  • Account upgrades and downgrades are available through the user dashboard.
  • Configuration changes are processed in real-time without service interruption.

7. Subscription Upgrades/Downgrades

  • Users can change subscription plans anytime from their account dashboard.
  • Upgrades are processed immediately with prorated billing adjustments.
  • Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle to avoid service disruption.
  • Plan changes are reflected in real-time within the user interface.

8. Downtime Compensation

  • If services are unavailable for over 12 consecutive hours, service credits may be issued.
  • Planned maintenance windows are announced 48 hours in advance.
  • Emergency downtime compensation is calculated based on affected service duration.
  • Service level agreements guarantee 99.5% uptime for paid subscriptions.

9. Data Responsibility

  • LeadsCruise is not liable for data loss caused by external factors beyond our control.
  • Users are responsible for maintaining secure API credentials and access tokens.
  • Regular data backups are performed, but users should maintain independent copies.
  • Third-party service disruptions may affect data synchronization temporarily.

10. Customer Support

All service delivery, access issues, or invoice-related concerns can be quickly resolved by contacting our dedicated support team.
