Privacy Policy

Learn how LeadsCruise protects and handles your personal data with transparency and security

Introduction

At LeadsCruise, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you access, subscribe to, or use our AI services.

1. Data Collection

  • We only collect data necessary for providing our services.
  • Information may include your name, email, billing details, and subscription activity.
  • We collect technical data such as IP addresses, browser information, and usage analytics.

2. Purpose of Data Use

  • To provide AI services and maintain platform performance.
  • To process billing, issue invoices, and deliver account-related communication.
  • To improve service quality and user experience.
  • To ensure platform security and prevent fraud.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.
  • No third-party marketing use of your data.
  • Data may be shared only when legally required or for payment processing.
  • We may share data with trusted service providers under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All sensitive billing details are stored securely with encryption.
  • Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
  • Regular security audits and updates are performed to maintain data integrity.

5. User Rights

  • You may request details of the information we hold about you.
  • You may request corrections or deletion of inaccurate data.
  • You have the right to withdraw consent for data processing at any time.
  • You may request data portability or account termination.

6. Cookies and Tracking

  • We may use cookies for login sessions and platform analytics.
  • Cookies do not collect personal information without your consent.
  • You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.
  • Essential cookies are necessary for platform functionality.

7. Retention of Data

  • Data is retained only as long as necessary for service provision.
  • Once accounts are terminated, associated data may be deleted within 30 days.
  • Billing records may be retained longer for legal and tax compliance.
  • You may request immediate data deletion upon account termination.

8. Privacy in AI Services

  • LeadsCruise does not use your data to build profiles or train third-party AI.
  • All AI service operations are kept confidential to your account.
  • Your lead data and business information remain private and secure.
  • AI processing is performed with strict data isolation measures.

9. Legal Compliance

  • LeadsCruise complies with applicable data protection laws and regulations.
  • Users must also ensure compliance with their local laws when using our platform.
  • We may update this policy to reflect changes in legal requirements.

10. Contact Regarding Privacy

  • For questions or requests, email us at support@leadscruise.com.
  • Privacy-related inquiries will be addressed within 5 business days.
  • You may also contact us via WhatsApp for urgent privacy concerns.
