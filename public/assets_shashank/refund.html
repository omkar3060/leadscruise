Cancellation and Refund Policy

Learn about our cancellation and refund policies for LeadsCruise AI services

Introduction

This Cancellation and Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for subscription cancellations and refund requests for LeadsCruise AI services. Please read this policy carefully to understand your rights and obligations.

1. Refund Guarantee

  • First-time users may request a refund within 3 days of purchase.
  • Refunds are not applicable after 3 days from the date of subscription activation.
  • The refund guarantee applies only to your initial subscription with LeadsCruise.

2. Conditions for Refunds

  • Refunds will be denied in cases of fraudulent activity or misuse of the platform.
  • Refund requests must be submitted to support@leadscruise.com.
  • All refund requests must include your account details and reason for the request.
  • Violation of our Terms of Service disqualifies users from refund eligibility.

3. Subscription Cancellations

  • Users can cancel subscriptions anytime via account settings.
  • Cancellation stops future billing but does not refund current charges.
  • Cancel anytime without penalty during your subscription period.
  • Cancellation requests can also be submitted via email support.

4. No Partial Refunds

  • Refunds are not issued for unused days or partial months of service.
  • Full subscription fees apply regardless of usage frequency.
  • Subscription benefits remain active until the end of the paid period.

5. Demo Subscriptions

  • Demo accounts are free, invite-only, and non-refundable.
  • No invoices are generated for demo subscriptions.
  • Demo access is limited to evaluation purposes only.
  • Demo subscriptions cannot be converted to refund requests.

6. Billing Cycle Rules

  • Once canceled, access remains until the end of the paid billing period.
  • No pro-rated refunds are provided for early cancellations.
  • Billing cycles are based on the original subscription start date.
  • Auto-renewal stops immediately upon cancellation.

7. Refund Timelines

  • Refunds, when approved, follow standard banking timelines of 5-10 business days.
  • Processing time may vary depending on your payment method and bank.
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method used for purchase.
  • Users will receive email confirmation once refund is processed.

8. Disputed Payments

  • Users must first raise disputes with LeadsCruise support before bank escalation.
  • Chargebacks initiated without prior contact may result in account suspension.
  • We work with users to resolve payment disputes amicably.
  • Documentation may be required to process dispute resolutions.

9. Non-Payment Consequences

  • Accounts with unresolved payments risk suspension or termination.
  • Failed payments result in a 10-day grace period to update payment information.
  • Services may be restricted during payment resolution periods.
  • Account termination may occur after repeated payment failures.

10. LeadsCruise Rights

  • LeadsCruise reserves the right to reject refund requests that do not meet the above terms.
  • We may request additional information to verify refund eligibility.
  • Policy modifications may be made with advance notice to users.
  • Final refund decisions rest with LeadsCruise management.
