Get in Touch

Our dedicated support team is committed to providing you with prompt, professional assistance. Whether you have technical questions, billing inquiries, or need help with our AI services, we're here to help you succeed.

1. Support Email

  • Reach us at support@leadscruise.com for all inquiries and assistance.
  • Include your account email and subscription details for faster resolution.
  • Attach relevant screenshots or error messages when reporting technical issues.
  • Our support team monitors this inbox 24/7 to ensure prompt responses.

2. Response Times

  • We aim to reply within 48 business hours for all standard inquiries.
  • Critical technical issues are prioritized and addressed within 4-6 hours.
  • Billing and account-related queries typically receive responses within 24 hours.
  • Business hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM IST.

3. Billing Queries

  • Contact our accounts team for GST invoice requests or billing discrepancies.
  • Payment failures and subscription renewal issues are resolved immediately.
  • Invoice downloads are available through your account dashboard under "Billing History".
  • Billing disputes must be raised within 30 days of the transaction date.

4. Refund Requests

  • Submit refund requests directly via email with your transaction details.
  • Include your subscription plan, purchase date, and reason for the refund request.
  • Refund requests can also be initiated through your account settings dashboard.
  • All refund requests are reviewed within 3-5 business days as per our refund policy.

5. Technical Issues

  • Report downtime, access errors, or system bugs to support immediately for priority handling.
  • Include detailed steps to reproduce the issue and your browser/device information.
  • API integration problems and lead synchronization issues are resolved within 2 hours.
  • Scheduled maintenance notifications are sent 48 hours in advance via email.

6. Dispute Resolution

  • All disputes should be raised first through our support team for internal resolution.
  • We follow a structured escalation process to ensure fair and timely dispute handling.
  • Service-related disputes are mediated by our customer success team.
  • Unresolved disputes may be escalated to management for final review and decision.

7. Account Termination Requests

  • To close your account, contact us via email or through your account dashboard.
  • Account closure requests are processed within 24-48 hours of confirmation.
  • Data export options are available before account termination if requested.
  • Subscription cancellations can be done immediately through account settings.

8. Fraud or Abuse Reports

  • Notify us immediately of any suspicious or abusive activity on our platform.
  • Security concerns and unauthorized access reports are handled with highest priority.
  • Include detailed information about the incident including timestamps and affected accounts.
  • Our security team investigates all reports within 2 hours of notification.

9. Feedback and Suggestions

  • We welcome user feedback to improve our platform and service quality.
  • Feature requests and improvement suggestions are reviewed by our product team.
  • User testimonials and success stories help us better serve our community.
  • Regular user surveys are conducted to gather valuable insights for platform enhancement.
